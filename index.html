creative fiefdoms

Art and design are neighbors who share many of the same interests, yet they don't seem to talk that much. Why?

Art can learn a lot from the pressures of commerce and audiences. Design can learn a lot about considering "success" as something other than pleasing only the very few who's personal biases influence the flow of money.

Where do you think you might be going? When does it make sense to bring your audience content they are not used to seeing?
